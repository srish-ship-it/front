About Us

We are building a platform designed especially for women to find safe, compatible roommates. At VibeNest, we prioritize your comfort, safety, and lifestyle preferences. Our goal is to make your roommate search not just easy, but empowering.

Our Mission

To create a safe and empowering space for women to find compatible roommates and housing with complete peace of mind.

Our Vision

To become the most trusted roommate matching platform for women, redefining urban living through empathy, safety, and technology.

"We started VibeNest after realizing how hard it was to find safe and understanding roommates as women in metro cities. Our goal is to remove that fear and bring ease to this journey."

– The VibeNest Team
